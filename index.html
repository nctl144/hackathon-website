Hack Stetson is the place for creativity. You have the chance to make your imagination come to life! There are endless possibilities to choose from such as web development/design, software engineering, hardware hacks, and more.

An Event Like No Other


Stetson University is proud to announce our third annual hackathon (a 30-hour overnight coding event) in DeLand, Florida. Our new name, the Greater Central Florida Tech Faire + Hackathon shows that we are planning a larger and more elaborate event than traditional hackathons. This year, we are significantly expanding the number of activities, faculty lectures, and prizes.

What/When/Where?


The Greater Central Florida Tech Faire + Hackathon will take place at Stetson University,

345 N Woodland Blvd, DeLand, FL 32724

on April 7 - 8th.

What if I get hungry?


We have more than enough food to keep you coding through the whole event! Special accommodations will be provided for those wil dietary restrictions.

What can I do?


The tech faire + hackathon offers tons of activities such as coding challenges, capture the flag, tech talks, workshops, and more!

Do I need to bring anything?


Bring your student ID, laptop, chargers, a change of clothes, your resume, and toiletries. A pillow and sleeping bag may be useful as well. You do not have to bring a team, idea, food, or any drinks. Please leave anything illegal at home.

Can I attend?


This event is open to any individual with a desire to learn and have a fun time.

How long is the event?


It lasts from Saturday Morning to Sunday afternoon! You may also drop in any time and leave if needed.
